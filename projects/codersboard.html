CodersBoard

Website for managing the organization

I’m part of CodersCrew, UX/UI Design team. CodersCrew is a non-profit organization operating in Wrocław. We are constantly developing to create a community where people are ready to work in scientific skills. There are lots of amazing people with big hearts, ready to help, answer any questions -ready to create amazing things together. We operate as an IT company, with management, PMs, marketers, designers, frontends and backends.

Overview

CodersCrew is getting bigger. We are communicating through Slack, mails, we are creating Google Documents, working in Jira, but there is a need for a platform to organize the organization and better managing. In the future, the site is to be made available to other organizations to help with managing. CodersCrew has its own company image and structure. Work on the website began on the part of UX, under the supervision of PM with the cooperation of programmers and with the help and supervision of the client (management board).

Approach

Thanks to the platform, the management board of the organization will be able to add members, check payment statuses for membership fees, and approve new projects. Members will be able to create their own profile, join projects or build their business history from initiatives in which they participate.

Timeline

Research/ Questionnaire/ Analyse

At the very beginning, CoderCrew members were surveyed using Google Surveys.

Main conclusions from the surveys:

- the section on projects, presentation of projects, description will be important
- users have trouble finding information on meeting dates,
- people do not know the leaders of the departments, they don’t know to whom to report on various matters
- a guide or mentor would be useful to introduce the new member to the association structure

Personas
